About Heart Disease Prediction System

The Heart Disease Prediction System is an AI-powered tool designed to assess the risk of heart disease based on user-provided health data. Our goal is to help individuals make informed health decisions by analyzing key parameters such as blood pressure, cholesterol levels, heart rate, and glucose levels.

Key Features

  • ✔ AI-based heart disease risk assessment
  • ✔ Fast and accurate predictions
  • ✔ User-friendly interface
  • ✔ Secure data handling

Our Team

Our team consists of data scientists, software engineers, and medical professionals working together to create a reliable and effective prediction system.
